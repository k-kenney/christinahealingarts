Hi, I'm Christina

Hello, welcome to Wellbeing Yoga with Christina. I'm happy you're here!

I'm passionate about using the tools of yoga to help people heal. It is my firm belief that everyone has the power to heal themselves.

We are all wounded in one way or another; it's part of the human condition. We are constantly being confronted with new opportunities to heal & grow..

Utilizing the tools of yoga, we can discern whether we continue on the current path set before us or re-evaluate familiar ways of being & meet the challenge of change head on.

The work is never done...until we die! BUT, the work can be liberating, fun & energizing!
