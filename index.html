Hi, I'm Christina

Hello! Welcome to Wellbeing Yoga with Christina. I'm happy you're here! I hope we get the opportunity to connect further.

If you've landed on my page, I assume it's because you're interested in learning more about yoga as it relates to overall wellness.

I'm passionate about using the tools of yoga to help people heal. We are all wounded in one way or another; it's part of the human condition. We are constantly being confronted with new opportunities to heal & grow.

Utilizing the tools of yoga we can discern whether we continue on the current path set before us or re-evaluate familiar ways of being & meet the challenge of change head on.

In other words, the ability to self-observe, the courage to act on our inner sense of values & beliefs, a deep capacity for love, and the human birthright of respect, understanding, caring and integrity. The work is never done...until we die! BUT, the work doesn't have to be painful, boring or exhausting. Infact, in can be liberating, fun & energizing!
