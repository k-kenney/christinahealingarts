What is a Local 1/2 Day Wellness Retreat?

You don't have to travel outside of your own city to experience the transformative healing benefits of a retreat. You also don't need to break the bank to attain balance in mind, body & soul. Additionally, you don't need to spend days or weeks away from home in some exotic country to find inner peace & connection.

Local

Our Local ½ Day Wellness Retreats are curated experiences and held right here in San Diego, CA. Each experience is unique & unlike the one before.

Support

Using various healing art tools such as yoga, reiki, meditation, creative journaling, sound healing & more. Experiencing a local ½ day wellness retreat is a great way to connect to others & receive ongoing relational support; we heal in community with the support of others. 

Community

The intention is to gather as a community in welcoming the next season of life; just as seasons change, we too experience transitions in our lives.
